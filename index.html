Shivangi Bishnoi

Economist turned Data Journalist

I am a data journalism student at Columbia University’s Graduate School of Journalism. I used to be an economist/ teacher and almost got a PhD in Economics. At a time when trust in news is low, I see science as a way to dispel the untruths. For this reason, I am keen to bring statistical methods to the newsroom. I am passionate about making science as well as data-driven stories more accessible to the public. I mostly look for stories at the intersection of climate, economics and public policy.
